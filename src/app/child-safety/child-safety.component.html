Our Commitment

EntreNos is deeply committed to ensuring a safe and positive experience for all our users. We have a zero-tolerance policy regarding child sexual exploitation and abuse (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM). Our platform is designed with safety as a priority, implementing robust measures to protect children and prevent any form of exploitation or harm within our community.

Reporting and Moderation

We empower our community to help maintain a safe environment through the following mechanisms:

  • In-App Reporting: Users can easily report harmful content, suspicious activities, or abusive behavior directly through the reporting tools available inside the app.
  • Proactive Review: All reports are prioritized and reviewed by the dedicated EntreNos team of moderators.
  • Swift Enforcement: Accounts found to be involved in any form of child abuse or exploitation are immediately and permanently removed from the platform.
  • Legal Action: We report illegal content and activities related to child safety to the relevant law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations.

Age Protection

EntreNos is a social platform intended for an adult audience. Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we suspect or identify an account belonging to a minor under 13, it will be restricted or removed immediately to maintain our safety standards.

Content Rules

We strictly prohibit and take immediate action against:

  • Any sexual content, imagery, or depictions involving minors.
  • Grooming or any attempt to establish an inappropriate connection with a minor.
  • The promotion or facilitation of child exploitation.
  • Any form of physical, emotional, or psychological abuse directed toward children.

Cooperation with Authorities

EntreNos fully cooperates with global law enforcement agencies and specialized child protection organizations (such as NCMEC). We comply with all legal requirements to report and provide information necessary for the investigation and prosecution of crimes against children.

Privacy & Enforcement

All reports concerning child safety are handled with the highest level of confidentiality and sensitivity. User data associated with these reports is used strictly for the purpose of internal investigation, safety enforcement, and legal compliance. We are dedicated to protecting the identity of reporters while ensuring the perpetrators are held accountable.
